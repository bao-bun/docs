## Quick Answers

### How do I edit my giveaway embeds?

Use custom content to design your giveaway embed, then apply it with `/giveaway embed <content_name>`. This lets you customize colors, fields, images, and more for your giveaway messages.

**Learn more:** [Configuration](./configuration)

### How do I add requirements to giveaways?

When creating a giveaway with `/giveaway start`, you can set requirements like role requirements through the interactive setup menu.

**Learn more:** [Management](./management)

### How do I give roles extra claim time / extra entries?

Create a preset with `/preset create`, then add roles with specific claim times and entries using `/preset add-role`. Apply the preset when starting your giveaway to give certain roles advantages.

**Learn more:** [Preset](./preset)
